Talk to me is a platform created by two guys who really want to learn English through interacting with others. Talk to me is the perfect place to practice your English skills and in the same way make a lot of connections with people around the world.

How does it work?

The app has the function of search a partner to talk with specific topics and when you said something wrong, the app corrects You. In "Talk to me" we believe that the society needs this app to speak in English without shame of making mistakeS but the confidence of being corrects in real time.
